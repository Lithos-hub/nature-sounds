F.A.Q.

You don't know how NATURE SOUNDS works? No problem. Keep reading!

Sounds Controls Volume Mixing Music
Each sound is represented by a significant icon. Below, you can consult the list of icons and their meaning with respect to the sounds they reproduce.
With the controls, you can select specific sounds within each context you have selected by means of the icon. That is, it is possible to play several sounds at once.
Of course, you can control the volume level of each sound separately. Volume at minimum is equivalent to mute that sound.
By combining the above options you can mix the different sounds to your liking to recreate a unique and personalized atmosphere.
On top of that, using the Spotify API you can listen to music along with your selected sounds. You can listen to music previously selected by me or choose the one you like best.
